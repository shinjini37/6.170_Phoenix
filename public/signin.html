Welcome to Phoenix

Phoenix is a social networking app designed for people who normally might find social situations anxiety-inducing or intimidating. We've taken several measures to lower the activation energy to making new friends and developing meaningful relationships. On Phoenix, you can escape the stigma of separate your gender, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, appearance, etc. if you so choose. You can choose to talk to people you are similar to, or who you are completely different from. We hope that Phoenix enables people to overcome the barriers of activation energy, social anxiety, depression, or whatever else may be keeping them from opening up and sharing their whole selves with the world.
